Getting Around NYC

How I navigated the city during my winter trip

The Subway

The NYC subway was my primary way of getting around. It's one of the largest public transit systems in the world with over 400 stations.

  • Running: 24/7, great for late night outings
  • Cost: $2.90 per ride with a MetroCard

Walking

NYC is a walking city. I walked everywhere - most Manhattan attractions are within walking distance.

  • Comfortable Shoes: A must - you'll walk a lot!
  • Winter Tips: Be careful on icy sidewalks in cold weather

Transportation Tips

  • Get a MetroCard: The easiest way to pay for subway rides
  • Walk When Possible: Many destinations are close together
  • Dress Warmly: Waiting for transit in winter is cold!
