NYC Neighborhoods

Exploring the diverse areas of New York City

The Five Boroughs

New York City is made up of five boroughs, each with its own unique character. During my visit, I had the chance to explore areas in Manhattan and Brooklyn.

Manhattan

Manhattan is the heart of NYC, where most famous attractions are located.

Midtown

Home to Times Square, Rockefeller Center, and the Empire State Building.

Upper East/West Side

Beautiful parks, museums, and residential areas near Central Park.

Brooklyn

Brooklyn offers a more relaxed vibe with diverse neighborhoods and great food.

DUMBO

Near the Brooklyn Bridge with amazing Manhattan views.

Williamsburg

Hip neighborhood with great restaurants and artistic culture.

Borough Quick Facts

Borough Population Known For
