Central Park

A peaceful winter wonderland in the heart of Manhattan

A Winter Wonderland

Central Park in winter is something truly magical. When I visited during Christmas 2025, the park was covered in snow, transforming this urban oasis into a peaceful winter wonderland.

My Personal Experience

I spent a whole morning exploring Central Park in the cold weather. The temperatures were around 30F, but the crisp air and beautiful scenery made it absolutely worth it. I walked along the paths, visited Bethesda Fountain, and enjoyed the quiet atmosphere away from the crowds.

Quick Facts

  • Founded: 1857
