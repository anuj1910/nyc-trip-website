Brooklyn Bridge

Breathtaking views connecting Manhattan and Brooklyn

Breathtaking Winter Views

Walking across the Brooklyn Bridge in the cold winter air was one of the highlights of my trip. The iconic suspension bridge offers stunning views of the NYC skyline.

My Experience

I decided to walk across the bridge on a cold afternoon. The temperatures were around 35F, and the wind was pretty strong, but the views were absolutely worth it. I started on the Manhattan side and walked toward Brooklyn.

Quick Facts

  • Location: East River
  • Total Length: 6,016 feet
  • Opened: May 24, 1883
  • Cost: Free to cross
