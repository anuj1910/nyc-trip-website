Times Square

The bright lights and energy of NYC's most famous intersection

My Experience at Times Square

Walking into Times Square for the first time during Christmas 2025 was absolutely magical. The bright neon lights, huge electronic billboards, and energy of the crowd made me feel like I was in the heart of New York City.

The cold winter air didn't stop thousands of people from crowding the streets. Everyone was in good spirits, taking photos and soaking in the festive atmosphere.

What Makes Times Square Special

  • The Lights: Thousands of neon signs creating a dazzling display
  • The Energy: Constant buzz of people and entertainment
  • Street Performers: Musicians and performers around every corner
  • Shopping: Flagship stores and souvenir shops

Quick Facts

  • Location: 42nd Street & Broadway
  • Subway: 42nd St-Port Authority
  • Open: 24 hours
  • Cost: Free to visit
